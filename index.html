Football Players Tracking from Multiple Cameras using Deep Learning

Sponsored by: KoraStats



The football player tracking system, leveraging several deep learning and computer vision technologies, aims to achieve precise tracking of players, address challenges such as player re-identification and occlusion. The integration of these models and algorithms expands the analytical capabilities, opening new possibilities in sports analytics and performance evaluation.
